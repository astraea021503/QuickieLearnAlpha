What Happens When Forms Are Unstyled?

Forms are essential for gathering user input on websites, but an unstyled form can create a frustrating experience. Imagine a form that has no spacing, borders, or clear labels. How would users feel trying to interact with such a form?

Let’s think about it: What is the difference between a well-styled form and one that is plain and unattractive? Why should you care about form design when it’s just about collecting data?
